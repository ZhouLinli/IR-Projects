Academic Achievement Center Review

Summary

  • Among the past 5 years (2018 Fall to 2022 Spring), AAC 103 course has an average enrollment of 50 students.
  • Average pass rate for AAC 103 course is 82%.
  • Students with higher than 2.0 semester GPA usually have higher pass rate in AAC103 than students with lower than 2.0 semester GPA.
  • An average of 73% students in AAC 103 course have a higher than 2.0 semester GPA.
  • Among those who pass AAC 103, 77% have a higher than 2.0 GPA and 23% do not have a good enough semester GPA. Among those who failed AAC 103, 58% still have a higher than 2.0 semester GPA (without succeeding in AAC 1O3 course).
  • Taking AAC 103 the second and third times does not improve pass rate or percentage of obtaining higher than 2.0 semester GPA.

Retaking AAC103
